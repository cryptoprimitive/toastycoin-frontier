Burnable Payments

Burnable Payments, or BPs, are tools for reliably outsourcing work wherein scammers are prevented from profiting by a payer-controlled, last-resort "burn" option. See a simple example or read about the game theory for more details.

BPs are non-proprietary; see the Factory contract and source code here.

Toastycoin Frontier

Toastycoin makes it easy to find and interact with BPs, or to create your own. This Frontier version is expected to undergo rapid development while providing stable basic features.

Follow the "Browse BPs" or "Create BP" links at the top left on the navigation bar, or check out the informational links on the right of the bar. You can use Toastycoin on the mainnet or on the Ropsten testnet by setting your provider (Metamask/Mist/Parity) to the appropriate network.

Toastycoin is also non-proprietary; see the source code here.

Other Tools

  • Toastycoin Beta is Toastycoin Frontier's predecessor and has some minor functional differences (detailed on its home page) compared to Toastycoin Frontier. Both interfaces follow the same Factory contract, and thus share the pool of BPs.
